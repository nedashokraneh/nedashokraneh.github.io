I am a fourth year Ph.D. student in Computer Science department at the Simon Fraser University, advised by Maxwell Libbrecht. My research is in modeling biological data with probabilistic models and machine learning techniques. In particular, I am interested in the application of Bayesian statistics and generalized models for count data in single-cell biology. My current project is on studying the chromatin 3D organization at single-cell resolution.
